Solving Project Euler Problems

These are a collection of scripts that will solve problems from Project Euler (at www.projecteuler.net). So far, these are scripted in the programming language Perl. There is a possibility that they are in Python. They are all written by Eric Leung.

  1. Multiples of 3 and 5 - "multiples3And5.pl" - 15 June 2013
  2. Even Fibonacci numbers - "evenFibo.pl" - 15 June 2013
  3. Largest prime factor - "largestPrimeFactor.pl" - 15 June 2013
  4. Largest palindrome product - "largestPalindromeProduct.pl - Solved 15 June 2013
  5. Smallest multiple - (Completed by hand) - 15 June 2013
  6. Sum square difference - sumSquareDiff.pl - 16 June 2013
  7. 10001st prime - "10001Prime.pl" - 16 June 2013
  8. Largest product in a series - "largestProductInSeries.pl" - 16 June 2013
  9. Special Pythagorean triplet - "specialPrythagoreanTrip.pl" - 18 June 2013
  10. Summation of primes - "sumOfPrimes.pl" - 18 June 2013
  11. Largest product in a Grid - (Used Excel to find largest numbers) - 28 December 2013
  12. Highly Divisible Triangle Number - "highDivisibleTriangleNum.pl" - 27 December 2013
  13. Large Sum - "largeSum.pl" - 27 December 2013
  14. Longest Collatz Sequence - "longestCollatzSeq.pl" - 29 December 2013
  15. Lattie Paths - (Used combinatorics) - 1 June 2014
  16. Number Letter Counts - "numLetterCounts.pl - 8 June 2014
  17. Maximum Path Sum 1 - N/A - N/A
  18. Counting Sundays - N/A - N/A
